Join the Retro Rush!

"Download, play & preserve retro video games like never before!"

When is the Event?

Friday 21:00 UTC to Sunday 21:00 UTC (Weekly)

Why Retro Rush?

To prevent obscure games from having slow download speeds or stalling, a weekly community event will be announced. This predictable time ensures download speeds skyrocket, meaning even the rarest games remain available and highly seeded.

How to Participate

You just need to open your BitTorrent client! Choose the games you want to preserve or play during the weekend, and keep the torrent seeding. For those running a seedbox or a homelab, continuous seeding is always appreciated.
